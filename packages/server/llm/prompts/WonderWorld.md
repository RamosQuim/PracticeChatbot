# WonderWorld Theme Park Knowledge Base

Welcome to WonderWorld! This document serves as the knowledge base for the WonderWorld chatbot assistant. It contains schedules, rides, attractions, pricing, and visitor information.

---

## 🎡 Park Overview

- **Name:** WonderWorld Theme Park
- **Location:** Orlando, Florida
- **Opening Year:** 2005
- **Theme:** Fantasy & Adventure for all ages

---

## 🕒 Park Schedule

- **Operating Hours:**
   - Monday–Friday: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM
   - Saturday–Sunday: 9:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Seasonal Events:**
   - Summer Splash Festival: June–August
   - Halloween Spooktacular: October

## 🎢 Major Rides & Attractions

### Thrill Rides

- **Dragon’s Flight:** High-speed roller coaster with loops and drops.
- **SkyDrop Tower:** Free-fall experience from 200 feet.
- **Cyclone Spin:** Extreme spinning ride for adrenaline seekers.

### Family Rides

- **WonderWheel:** Giant Ferris wheel with panoramic views.
- **Magic Carousel:** Classic merry-go-round with fantasy creatures.
- **River Rapids Adventure:** Water ride through caves and waterfalls.

### Kids’ Zone

- **Fairy Tale Train:** Gentle ride through storybook scenes.
- **Mini Coaster:** Beginner-friendly roller coaster.
- **Playland:** Interactive playground with slides and climbing towers.

---

## 🎭 Shows & Entertainment

- **Daily Parade:** 3:00 PM along Main Street.
- **WonderWorld Live Stage:** Musical performances at 5:00 PM and 7:00 PM.
- **Fireworks Spectacular:** Nightly at closing time.

## 🍔 Dining Options

- **Fantasy Feast Hall:** Buffet-style dining.
- **Adventure Snacks:** Quick bites and drinks.
- **Sweet Treats Corner:** Ice cream, cotton candy, and pastries.

---

## 🛍️ Shopping

- **WonderWorld Gift Shop:** Souvenirs, plush toys, and apparel.
- **Magic Market:** Themed merchandise and collectibles.

---

## 📌 Visitor Information

- **Tickets:** Available online and at the gate.
- **FastPass:** Skip-the-line option for major rides.
- **Accessibility:** Wheelchair rentals and accessible ride options.
- **Guest Services:** Located near the main entrance.
- **Parking:** $20 per vehicle, free for season pass holders.
- **Outside Food Policy:** No outside food or drinks allowed, except baby food and medical needs.

---

## 🚨 Emergency & Safety

- First Aid stations located in each zone.
- Security staff available throughout the park.
- Lost & Found near Guest Services.

---

## 💡 Tips for Visitors

- Arrive early to enjoy popular rides with shorter wait times.
- Use the WonderWorld mobile app for maps and real-time updates.
- Stay hydrated and take breaks in shaded areas.
